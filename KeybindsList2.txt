/Keyboard_Left Control\ + /Keyboard_D\ to duplicated the selected object.
/Keyboard_Left Control\ + /Keyboard_S\ to save the level.
/Keyboard_Left Control\ + /Keyboard_P\ to play the level.
/Keyboard_Left Control\ + /Keyboard_H\ to hide/show object categories buttons.
/Keyboard_Left Control\ + /Keyboard_Z\ to undo the last executed action (move/rotate/delete).

/Keyboard_G\ to switch between global/local move arrow gizmos.
/Keyboard_Left Control\ + /Keyboard_O\ to open the Global Properties Panel.
/Keyboard_Space\ (while selecting an object) to change the "Set Active At Start" toggle.
